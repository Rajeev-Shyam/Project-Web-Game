This game is about a player running from destruction which is awaiting on the earth.

The player has to evade the holes which appear in front of him as hes running and there will be walls which can block him.

The player has to collect the yellow coins to gain score.

You can use the up,down,left and right key to move the player.

Try your best to not fall into the holes and you can also check if you are in the Leaderboards.

Thank you for playing!
